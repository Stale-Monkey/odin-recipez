Roasted Cauliflower

This easy cauliflower recipe is perfectly flavored with garlic and Parmesan cheese. Your family will enjoy this tender, oven-roasted cauliflower with any meal. My 11-year-old son loves this! Add more spices and herbs to suit your taste.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 large head cauliflower, seperated into florets
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
